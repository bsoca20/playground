Root Causes · Why This Happens
Six Structural Reasons Launches Miss

Most underperformance is not due to clinical failure, but to commercial and organizational factors that disconnect the asset from real-world decision making and patient journeys.

01
Market Access + Pricing
Underestimating payer evidence needs, price elasticity and local HTA timelines.
02
Weak Differentiation
"Good drug" but insufficiently differentiated in crowded classes or vs. standard of care.
03
Fragmented Insights
Disconnected research vendors, siloed teams and slow feedback loops.
04
Forecast Errors
Over-optimistic investor narratives that set unreachable early targets.
05
Omnichannel Gaps
Inconsistent messaging, poor sequencing and inadequate digital engagement.
06
Patient Blind Spots
Unseen barriers in diagnosis, access, affordability and adherence.
The real diagnosis
Recent underperformance reflects a broken launch system, not just a weak launch campaign. The market changed faster than the operating model did. ZS Biopharma 2025; IQVIA Modern Launch 2025; Deloitte Customer Engagement 2025

The Strategic Response · Winning Launch Architecture
If underperformance is structural —
the response must be structural too.

Different timing. Different ownership. A different definition of value creation. Here is what the evidence says winning launches do differently.

01 · Earlier, Not Later
Start Access & Evidence at Phase II
Payer evidence, reimbursement scenarios, and patient support design built before approval — not bolted on at the end.
02 · System, Not Product
Focus on System Value
Reduce burden for healthcare systems — not only improve clinical endpoints. Payers respond to system economics.
03 · Capability, Not Event
Treat Launch as a Capability
Dedicated launch excellence teams, playbooks, and learning loops across brands — not rebuilt from scratch each time.
04 · Optichannel by Design
Optimal Routes, Not Maximum Presence
Human + AI orchestration built from the HCP journey outward. Right channel, right message, right moment.
05 · Real-Time Intelligence
Unified Launch Dashboards
Track access, behavior, and perception in near real time. Leading indicators, not lagging sales reports.
06 · Design for Post-Rx
Convert Prescriptions to Starts
Affordability, prior auth, benefit verification, and first fill treated as launch-critical capabilities from day one.
The most important shift: from "we've already launched" to "we are building a launch system." Launch excellence is not a 90-day sprint — it is an ongoing capability that requires ecosystemic thinking, shared ownership, and a new leadership mindset.
